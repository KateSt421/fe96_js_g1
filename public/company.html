Expert Plant Care Tips

Watering Best Practices

Most houseplants die from overwatering rather than underwatering. Always check the soil moisture before watering - insert your finger about an inch into the soil, and only water if it feels dry. Different plants have different watering needs, so learn about each species' specific requirements.

Light Requirements

Understanding your home's light conditions is crucial for plant success. South-facing windows provide bright direct light, east and west windows offer medium light, and north windows provide low light. Match your plants to the available light or supplement with grow lights for light-loving plants in darker spaces.
